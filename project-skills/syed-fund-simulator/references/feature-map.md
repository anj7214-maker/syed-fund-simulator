## Major Enhancements Already Added

- Approved-email access control through Clerk and Vercel env vars.
- Dynamic AI Copilot with contextual prompts and chat-style panel.
- Suggested Operational Questions Engine.
- Sandbox and Live Mode naming.
- Scenario Simulation tab with editable workbench patterns.
- Manual data edit and submit/recalculate workflow.
- Download current data and before/after impact report controls.
- Corporate Actions editable and linked to accruals, GL, and NAV.
- Trade Blotter and General Ledger tie-out for cash movement.
- Investor-level management fee schedule.

- Institutional multi-sheet NAV Pack export.
- Multi-structure fund comparison and structure NAV bridge.
